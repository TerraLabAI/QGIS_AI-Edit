    Select an area, write what you want -> get the result back as a georeferenced layer

    • Works on any data on your map: rasters (GeoTIFF, JPG, PNG), vector layers, OpenStreetMap, and online basemaps (WMS, XYZ tiles)

    • Guide the result: add reference images or layers, sketch directly on the map, and compare before/after with a slider

    • Vectorize results: turn a generated map into clean vector polygons (buildings, parcels, land cover) ready for SHP or GeoJSON

    • 100+ ready-made prompt templates, or write your own

    • One AI model for almost anything: describe a task and it handles it, from urban planning and solar studies to crop mapping, archaeology, or restoring old maps

    • Universal: Windows, macOS, Linux. Powered by the Best Generative AI Edit model (From Google)
